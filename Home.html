ABOUT US

Welcome to Food Truck! Established in 2023, our food truck has been on a mission to serve delicious food to the vibrant communities of Ashok nagar, Chennai. You can find us cruising through the city, bringing mouthwatering flavors right to your doorstep. Our dedication to quality and taste has made us a beloved landmark wherever we go.
